To see the world is a treat for anybody. Plus, I'm of the mindset that seeing and experiencing other cultures while interacting with others is something that we shouldn't take for granted. Our planet is a beautiful place that deserves to be admired.

Complete a project car

Honestly. I love cars. Usually they're just seen as a means from point A to point B, and while yes, that's their purpose, they're so much more than that. The sounds of the engine, the feel of the steering wheel, the connection to the road, man and machine working as one. While I am being a bit artsy, I do think that cars themselves are works of art and show off how amazing human engineering and ingenuity can be, and in that vein, I would love to work on and Complete my own project car one day.
